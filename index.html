How to make an Omelette
Step 1: Beat 2 eggs in a bowl
Step 2: Melt half a stick of butter in the pan, over medium-low heat
Step 3: Pour the eggs in the pan
Step 4: When eggs start to bubble, add any additional ingredient
Step 5: Gently lift one side of the omelette and flip
Step 6: Serve on a dish and enjoy!
